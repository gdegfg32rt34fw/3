Our Story

From the bustling streets of Tehran to the heart of Sydney, our family has been crafting authentic Persian desserts for over three decades, bringing the rich traditions of Middle Eastern patisserie to Australia.

Our Heritage Timeline

A journey through time, tradition, and taste

1987 - Tehran Beginnings

Our family's patisserie journey began in the heart of Tehran, where we established our first shop specializing in traditional Persian desserts. The bustling markets and rich culinary culture of Iran's capital provided the perfect foundation for our craft.

1
2

1995 - Perfecting the Craft

Through years of dedication and passion, we perfected our signature recipes for baklava, Persian cookies, and cream pastries. Our reputation grew as we became known for the finest traditional desserts in our neighborhood.

2005 - Australian Dream

With a vision to share our heritage with a new audience, our family made the bold decision to move to Sydney, Australia. We brought with us not just our recipes, but our passion for authentic Persian patisserie culture.

3
4

2010 - Sydney Opening

Saffran Patisserie opened its doors in Baulkham Hills, bringing authentic Persian desserts to the local community. The response was overwhelming as customers discovered the rich flavors and delicate craftsmanship of our traditional sweets.

2020 - Modern Era

Embracing modern technology while staying true to our traditions, we partnered with delivery platforms to bring our desserts to homes across Sydney. Our commitment to quality and authenticity remains unchanged.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Authenticity

We preserve traditional recipes and techniques passed down through generations, ensuring every dessert maintains its authentic Persian character and cultural significance.

Passion

Every dessert is crafted with love and attention to detail. Our passion for Persian patisserie culture drives us to create sweets that bring joy and create lasting memories.

Quality

We source only the finest ingredients, from premium nuts and spices to authentic Persian rose water, ensuring every bite delivers exceptional quality and flavor.

The Art of Persian Patisserie

Persian dessert making is an art form that requires patience, skill, and deep understanding of flavor harmonies. Each dessert tells a story of cultural heritage and culinary mastery.

From the delicate layering of phyllo in our baklava to the precise balance of rose water and spices in our cookies, every step is performed with meticulous attention to detail. This dedication ensures that each dessert not only tastes exceptional but also honors the rich traditions of Persian cuisine.
